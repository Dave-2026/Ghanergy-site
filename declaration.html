Declaration

A Constitution for Sustainable Infrastructure Leadership in Ghana

Preamble

Ghanergy is established to transform waste into value, infrastructure into resilience, and investment into national progress. We operate at the intersection of environmental necessity, energy security and economic development.

Vision

To be the leading developer and operator of integrated Waste-to-Energy infrastructure in West Africa.

Mission

The first step is the development of the Waste-to-Energy Plant for Greater Accra, Ghana:

  • Convert ~3,000 tonnes per day of waste into energy
  • Deliver ~60 MW of baseload power
  • Divert >90% of waste from landfill
  • Structure bankable, transparent projects

Core Principles

  • National alignment
  • Bankability without compromise
  • Execution certainty
  • Environmental integrity
  • Economic discipline
  • Ghanaian ownership
  • Accountability and governance

Commitment

Ghanergy commits to delivering infrastructure that improves public health, provides reliable power, and creates long-term value for Ghana and its people.
