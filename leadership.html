Leadership

Ghanergy is led by an experienced executive team combining international energy sector expertise, legal and commercial structuring capability, and Ghanaian project leadership, with a focus on disciplined project delivery and long-term operational performance.

The leadership team is responsible for project development, stakeholder engagement, financing strategy and execution of the Greater Accra Waste-to-Energy Project.
